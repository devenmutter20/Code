About Us


Welcome to The Take Me Back Cafe.

We are a small cafe located in Paris, Tennessee.

Our cafe is located on 302 W Wood St.

We have many options to choose from, and you can view the most popular items on the Price & Menu page.

We serve breakfast all day, which many people enjoy having that option.

We are family-friendly and wheelchair accessible.

We have moderate service, friendly waitresses, and friendly cooks.

Our cafe has many memorabilias of Elvis Presley, The Beatles, Tennessee Volunteers, and many other celebrities.

Many people love the food and service, we get many compliments throughout the day.

The cafe is very people-friendly.
